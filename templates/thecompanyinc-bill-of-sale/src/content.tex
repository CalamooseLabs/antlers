This Bill of Sale and Assignment of Assets (this ``Agreement'') is made effective as of
\effectiveDate\ (the ``Effective Date''), by and between:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, label=(\arabic*)]
	\item \sellerName, \sellerType\ with an address at \sellerAddress\ (``Seller''); and
	\item \companyName, a \companyState\ corporation, with an address at \companyAddress\
	(the ``Company'').
\end{enumerate}

\noindent Seller and the Company may be referred to individually as a ``Party'' and
collectively as the ``Parties.''

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, label=\arabic*.]
	\item \textbf{Sale and Transfer.}\enspace For good and valuable consideration, the receipt
	and sufficiency of which are acknowledged, Seller hereby sells, transfers, conveys, assigns,
	and delivers to the Company, and the Company hereby purchases and accepts from Seller, all of
	Seller's right, title, and interest in and to the Assets (defined below), effective as of the
	Effective Date, subject to the terms of this Agreement.

	\item \textbf{Assets.}
	\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, label=\arabic{enumi}.\arabic*]
		\item \uline{Assets}. ``Assets'' means the personal property and other property described
		on Exhibit A (including, as applicable, accessories, attachments, manuals, records, and
		other items primarily related thereto), together with all of Seller's right, title, and
		interest in and to the same, to the extent transferable and assignable.

		\item \uline{Excluded Assets}. The following are excluded from the Assets (the ``Excluded
		Assets''): \excludedAssets.
	\end{enumerate}

	\item \textbf{Purchase Price.}\enspace The purchase price for the Assets is \purchasePrice\
	(the ``Purchase Price''), allocated among the Assets as set forth on Exhibit A (or as
	otherwise agreed in writing by the Parties).

	\item \textbf{Delivery; Transfer of Possession.}\enspace Seller shall deliver the Assets to
	the Company on \deliveryDate, at \deliveryPlace, or as otherwise agreed in writing by the
	Parties.

	\item \textbf{Assignment of Related Rights.}\enspace To the extent any of the Assets include
	assignable rights (including, if applicable, warranties, service contracts, maintenance
	agreements, or other rights related to the Assets), Seller hereby assigns such rights to the
	Company effective as of the Effective Date, to the extent assignable without third-party
	consent. If any third-party consent is required, the Parties shall cooperate in good faith to
	obtain such consent.

	\item \textbf{Seller Representations and Warranties.}\enspace Seller represents and warrants
	to the Company that, as of the Effective Date:
	\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, label=\arabic{enumi}.\arabic*]
		\item \uline{Title; Authority}. Seller is the lawful owner of the Assets and has full
		right, power, and authority to enter into this Agreement and to transfer the Assets to the
		Company.

		\item \uline{No Liens}. The Assets are transferred free and clear of all liens, security
		interests, pledges, encumbrances, claims, and charges of any kind, except: \permittedLiens.

		\item \uline{No Conflicts}. The execution, delivery, and performance of this Agreement by
		Seller do not violate any agreement binding on Seller or any applicable law.
	\end{enumerate}

	\item \textbf{Disclaimer; As-Is Transfer.}\enspace EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH IN THIS
	AGREEMENT, SELLER MAKES NO REPRESENTATION OR WARRANTY OF ANY KIND, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, WITH
	RESPECT TO THE ASSETS, INCLUDING ANY IMPLIED WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A
	PARTICULAR PURPOSE, TITLE, OR NON-INFRINGEMENT. THE ASSETS ARE TRANSFERRED ``AS IS,'' ``WHERE
	IS,'' AND ``WITH ALL FAULTS,'' AND THE COMPANY HAS RELIED ON ITS OWN INSPECTION AND
	INVESTIGATION OF THE ASSETS.

	\item \textbf{Further Assurances.}\enspace Seller shall execute and deliver such additional
	documents and instruments and take such further actions as the Company may reasonably request
	to evidence, perfect, or effectuate the transfer and assignment contemplated by this
	Agreement.

	\item \textbf{Taxes; Fees.}\enspace Any sales, use, transfer, personal property, documentary,
	filing, or similar taxes or fees arising from or related to the transactions contemplated by
	this Agreement shall be paid by: \taxParty.

	\item \textbf{Governing Law; Venue.}\enspace This Agreement shall be governed by and construed
	in accordance with the laws of the State of \companyState, without regard to its conflict of
	laws principles. Any action or proceeding arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be
	brought in the state or federal courts located in \venueCounty, \companyState, and each Party
	irrevocably submits to the jurisdiction and venue of such courts.

	\item \textbf{Miscellaneous.}
	\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, label=\arabic{enumi}.\arabic*]
		\item \uline{Entire Agreement}. This Agreement (including Exhibit A) constitutes the entire
		agreement between the Parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior
		and contemporaneous understandings and agreements, whether written or oral.

		\item \uline{Amendments}. This Agreement may be amended or modified only by a written
		instrument executed by both Parties.

		\item \uline{Counterparts; Electronic Signatures}. This Agreement may be executed in
		counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall
		constitute one and the same instrument. Signatures delivered by electronic means (including
		PDF) shall be deemed effective for all purposes.

		\item \uline{Severability}. If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or
		unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

		\item \uline{Assignment}. The Company may assign this Agreement without Seller's consent to
		any affiliate or successor (by merger, reorganization, sale of substantially all assets, or
		otherwise). Seller may not assign this Agreement without the Company's prior written
		consent.
	\end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}
