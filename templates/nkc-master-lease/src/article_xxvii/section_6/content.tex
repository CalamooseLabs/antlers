\label{sec:force-majeure}
In the event that either party, of the \parties, thereto shall be delayed or hindered in or prevented from the performance of any act required hereunder by reason of strikes, lockouts, labor troubles, inability to procure materials, failure of power, restrictive government laws or regulations, riots, insurrection , war , limitation or interference of governmental authorities or agents, governmental pre-emption or prescription or other controls in connection with a national or other public emergency, national emergency, acts of God, acts of enemies, terrorism, bioterrorism, civil commotion, actual or threatened health emergency, including, without limitation, epidemic, pandemic, famine, disease, plague quarantine, and other health risks, or other reason of a like nature, that materially negatively impacts \lessee's use of the \premises, not at the fault of the party delayed in performing work or doing as required under the terms of this \lease, then performance of such act shall be excused for the period of delay and the period for the performance of any such act shall be extended for a period equivalent to the period of such delay. The provisions of this Section~\ref{sec:force-majeure} shall not operate to excuse \lessee from the prompt payment of \monthlyRent, or any other  payments required under the terms of this \lease.
