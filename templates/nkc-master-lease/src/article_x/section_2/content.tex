Except with respect to any \permittedTransfer (as set forth above), \lessor's consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld, conditioned or delayed, shall be required if the \lessee desires to sublet the \premises, or any part thereof, nor assign this \lease, nor permit any transfer by operation of law of the interest in the \premises acquired through this \lease, to a proposed transferee who intends to use the \premises for a use other than the \permittedUse (each, a ``\dtdef{consentTransfer}''). If \lessee desires \lessor's consent to any \consentTransfer, \lessee shall notify \lessor in writing (the ``\consentTransfer Notice'') of any proposed \consentTransfer, and such \consentTransfer Notice shall include: (i) the proposed effective date of the \consentTransfer; (ii) a description of the portion of the \premises to be transferred; (iii) the name and address of the proposed transferee; (iv) an executed copy of the proposed assignment, sublease, or other transfer documentation effectuating the proposed \consentTransfer; and (v) financial statements of transferee. In the event \lessor fails to respond to \lessee's \consentTransfer Notice within ten (10) business days, \lessor shall be deemed to have consented to the \consentTransfer that is the subject of the \consentTransfer Notice.
