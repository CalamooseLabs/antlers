A ``\dtdef{totalLoss}'' shall be deemed to have occurred if (i) the \building is so damaged by fire or other casualty that the estimated cost to repair same amounts to fifty percent (50\%) or more of the total estimated construction cost of the entire \building; (ii) the \building is so damaged by fire or other casualty that \lessor, in its sole discretion, decides to demolish and not immediately rebuild same; or (iii) the \premises is damaged by fire or other casualty during the last twelve (12) months of the \termOfLease hereof that the estimated cost to repair same amount to fifty percent (50\%) or more of the total estimated construction cost of the entire \premises. Any other casualty loss not amounting to a \totalLoss shall be deemed a ``\dtdef{partialLoss}''.
