\label{sec:real-estate-taxes}
From and after the \rentCommencementDate and throughout the balance of the \termOfLease, \lessee will pay, as \additionalRent hereunder, the \lesseesPercentage of the real estate taxes payable during each calendar year falling within such period with respect to the \commonAreas and the \building in which the \premises are located, including the parcel of land on which such \building is constructed and all other improvements thereto. Such real estate taxes shall be equitably prorated if the \rentCommencementDate occurs on a day other than the first day of a calendar month. Such real estate taxes shall be paid on an estimated basis, monthly in advance, as \additionalRent, in accordance with Section~\ref{sec:estimated-payments}, below. In the event \lessor successfully protests the real estate taxes attributable to the \building, \lessor will give \lessee its \lesseesPercentage of the refund (after payment of \lesseesPercentage of the reasonable and customary fees incurred in connection with such protest, provided that such fees are on a contingency fee basis only). In the event \lessor receives a discount on its real estate taxes attributable to the \building, \lessor will provide \lessee with its \lesseesPercentage of the discount. Provided \lessee has paid \lesseesPercentage of real estate taxes to \lessor in accordance with this Section~\ref{sec:real-estate-taxes} and Section~\ref{sec:estimated-payments} below, \lessee will not be responsible for any penalties due to \lessor's late payments of taxes. The term ``taxes'' or ``real estate taxes'' shall not include any net income, capital, stock, succession, transfer, gift, estate, or inheritance tax.
