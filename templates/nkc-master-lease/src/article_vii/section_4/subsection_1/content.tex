Throughout the \termOfLease, commencing on the \deliveryDate, \lessee shall contract directly with the public utilities furnishing utilities (such as electric) which if separately metered to the \premises, shall pay such utility providers directly and promptly when due. \lessee shall be responsible for \lesseesPercentage of the utility expenses which if shared meter to the \premises and shall pay monthly, directly to \lessor. The costs of any common utility consumed on the \building or \commonAreas shall be included within the \additionalRent, of which \lessee shall pay \lesseesPercentage. The \rule{2cm}{0.4pt} supplied to the \building will be considered a common utility, will be included in the \additionalRent pursuant to \lesseesPercentage, and shall not need to be separately metered to the \premises. \lessee shall reimburse \lessor for \lesseesPercentage of the cost of such common utilities on an estimated basis monthly, in advance, as provided for in Section~\ref{sec:estimated-payments}, below, as \additionalRent.
