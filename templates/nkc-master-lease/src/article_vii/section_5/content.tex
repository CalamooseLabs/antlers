\label{sec:hvac}
Throughout the \termOfLease, \lessor will engage a reputable and experienced firm for the purpose of periodically inspecting and maintaining the heating ventilation, and air conditioning equipment located on the \building (the ``\hvacMaintenance''). \lessee shall reimburse the \lesseesPercentage of the cost of such maintenance and inspection on an estimated basis, monthly in advance as provided for in Section~\ref{sec:estimated-payments}, below, as \additionalRent.
