\label{sec:option-i}
\lessee shall have the option (``Option \#1'') to extend the initial \termOfLease for an additional period of \optionILengthText\ (the ``Option Term \#1''), provided that \lessee notifies \lessor not less than nine (9) months prior to the expiration of the initial \termOfLease (the ``Option \#1 Notification Date''). In the event \lessee exercises Option \#1, the \termOfLease shall be extended for the Option Term \#1, at a \dtdef{fairMarketValue} for the \premises upon the terms and conditions expressed in this \lease and ``\termOfLease'', as used in this \lease, shall include Option Term \#1. If \lessee fails to give written notice to \lessor by, or before, the Option \#1 Notification Date, the \lease shall expire on the \expirationDate. \fairMarketValue for Option Term \#1 shall be determined in accordance with Section~\ref{sec:fair-market-value} below.
