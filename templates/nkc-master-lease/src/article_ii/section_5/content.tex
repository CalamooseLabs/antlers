\label{sec:option-ii}
\lessee shall have the option (``Option \#2'') to extend the initial \termOfLease (as previously extended by Option Term \#1) for an additional period of \optionIILengthText\ (the ``Option Term \#2''), provided that the \lessee notifies \lessor not less than nine (9) months prior to the expiration of the Option Term \#1 (the ``Option \#2 Notification Date''). In the event \lessee exercises Option \#2, the \termOfLease shall be extended for the Option Term \#2, at a \fairMarketValue for the \premises upon the terms and conditions expressed in this \lease, and ``\termOfLease'', as used in this \lease, shall include Option Term \#2. If \lessee fails to give written notice by, or before, the Option \#2 Notification Date, the \lease shall expire on the last day of the Option Term \#1. \fairMarketValue for Option Term \#2 shall be determined in accordance with Section~\ref{sec:fair-market-value} below.
