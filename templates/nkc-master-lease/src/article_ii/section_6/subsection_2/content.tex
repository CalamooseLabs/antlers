If \lessee provides \lessor with its written notice of exercise pursuant to Section~\ref{sec:option-i} and/or Section~\ref{sec:option-ii} above, respectively, \lessor shall calculate and inform \lessee of the \fairMarketValue within ten (10) business days of \lessor's receipt of \lessee's notice to extend the \termOfLease. Within ten (10) days of \lessee's receipt of \lessor's calculation, \lessee shall notify \lessor of whether \lessee agrees with \lessor's calculation. If \lessee disagrees with \lessor's calculation, \lessor and \lessee shall commence negotiations to agree upon the \fairMarketValue. If, despite using good faith efforts, \lessor and \lessee are unable to reach agreement as to the \fairMarketValue within sixty (60) days after the date that \lessee first deliver its written notice of exercise to \lessor (the ``\dtdef{negotiationPeriod}''), the option shall expire.
