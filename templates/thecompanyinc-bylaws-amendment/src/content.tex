\bigskip

This Amendment to the Bylaws (this ``Amendment'') of \companyName, a \companyState\
corporation (the ``Corporation''), is adopted effective as of \effectiveDate, pursuant to
Article XI of the Bylaws of the Corporation (the ``Bylaws'') and \S~\statuteCite\ of the
Colorado Business Corporation Act.

\bigskip

\formaltext{RECITALS}

\noindent WHEREAS, the Bylaws were duly adopted to govern the regulation and management of the
affairs of the Corporation; and

\smallskip

\noindent WHEREAS, Article XI of the Bylaws provides that the Bylaws may be made, altered,
amended, or repealed by the shareholders or the board of directors of the Corporation; and

\smallskip

\noindent WHEREAS, the board of directors and the shareholders of the Corporation have
determined that it is in the best interests of the Corporation to amend the Bylaws as set
forth herein.

\bigskip

\noindent NOW, THEREFORE, the Bylaws are hereby amended as follows:

\smallskip

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*, label=\arabic*.]
	\item \textbf{Amendment.}\enspace \amendedSection\ of the Bylaws is hereby amended and
	restated in its entirety to read as follows:

	\smallskip
	\begin{quote}
		\amendedText
	\end{quote}

	\item \textbf{No Other Changes.}\enspace Except as expressly amended hereby, the Bylaws
	remain in full force and effect and are hereby ratified and confirmed in all respects.

	\item \textbf{Governing Law.}\enspace This Amendment shall be governed by, and construed in
	accordance with, the laws of the State of \companyState, including the Colorado Business
	Corporation Act.
\end{enumerate}

\bigskip

\formaltext{CERTIFICATE OF SECRETARY}

\noindent The undersigned, being the duly elected and acting Secretary of \companyName, hereby
certifies that the foregoing Amendment to the Bylaws was duly adopted in accordance with
Article XI of the Bylaws and the Colorado Business Corporation Act, and that the same has not
been modified or rescinded and remains in full force and effect as of \effectiveDate.

\bigskip
